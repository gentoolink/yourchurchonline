Frequently Asked Questions

What is your statement of faith?

We align with the historic, orthodox Christian faith as expressed in creeds like the Nicene Creed and the Apostles' Creed. We serve churches from a variety of evangelical denominations and are committed to reflecting your church's specific theological voice and doctrine in all content we produce.

Can we cancel at any time?

Yes. All our plans are month-to-month. We believe in earning your trust every month and don't require long-term contracts. We ask for 30 days' notice to ensure a smooth transition of any assets.

Who owns the website and content?

You do. Your church retains 100% ownership of your domain, website, and all content we create for you. If you ever decide to leave, we'll help you package and transfer everything to your new provider.

How does the sermon content process work?

You provide us with the audio or video file of your weekly sermon. We transcribe it, write a summary blog post, pull out key quotes for social media, and draft the shepherding email. Everything is sent to you or a designated staff member for approval before it's published.
